Electrical Services in Mossel Bay

4C Electrical is a licensed, COC-certified electrical contractor working across Mossel Bay, Hartenbos and Great Brak River. We deliver new installations, fault finding, VSD commissioning and solar PV for residential and commercial clients.

Mossel Bay is part of 4C Electrical's core Garden Route service area. We work right across the town and its neighbouring areas — including Hartenbos and Great Brak River — handling everything from a single faulty circuit to the complete electrical installation on a new build or commercial fit-out.

Every installation we complete in Mossel Bay is carried out by a registered electrician to SANS 10142, the South African standard for the wiring of premises. A Certificate of Compliance (COC) is issued for every qualifying job, so you have the legal documentation needed when selling property, connecting a new supply, or signing off after major electrical work.

The four core services we offer in Mossel Bay are:

  • New electrical installations for residential, commercial and agricultural buildings.
  • Electrical fault finding for tripping breakers, dead circuits, wiring faults and earth leakage issues.
  • VSD installations — Variable Speed Drives for motor control on pumps, fans, compressors and conveyors.
  • Solar installations — both grid-tied and off-grid PV systems, with the relevant Eskom and municipal paperwork.

From our Garden Route base we also serve George, Knysna, Wilderness, Sedgefield and Albertinia, so we can group jobs across the coast efficiently. English and Afrikaans are both spoken. Payment is accepted in ZAR by cash or EFT, and our business hours are Monday to Friday, 07:00–17:00.
