Electrical Services in Knysna

4C Electrical is a licensed, COC-certified electrical contractor serving Knysna and the surrounding Garden Route — Sedgefield and Wilderness included. We cover new installations, fault finding, VSD commissioning and solar PV.

Knysna is one of the main towns in our Garden Route service area. From homes and guesthouses to commercial premises, we handle electrical work right across town and travel out to nearby Sedgefield and Wilderness. Solar installations have been a particularly active line of work for us in Knysna, including grid-tied systems for guesthouses.

Every installation we sign off is completed by a registered electrician to SANS 10142, the South African standard for the wiring of premises. We issue a Certificate of Compliance (COC) for every qualifying installation — the document you need when selling a property, connecting a new electricity supply, or completing major electrical work.

The four core services available to Knysna clients are:

  • New electrical installations for new builds, renovations and commercial properties.
  • Electrical fault finding — tripping breakers, dead circuits, earth leakage and wiring faults.
  • VSD (Variable Speed Drive) installations for pumps, fans, compressors and conveyor systems.
  • Solar installations — grid-tied systems that feed excess back to the municipality, and off-grid systems with batteries for full energy independence.

Our wider Garden Route coverage also takes in George, Mossel Bay, Hartenbos, Great Brak River and Albertinia. English and Afrikaans are both spoken. Payment is in ZAR by cash or EFT, and we work Monday to Friday, 07:00–17:00.
