Electrical Services in George

4C Electrical is a COC-certified electrical contractor based in the Garden Route, with George as a core service town. We handle new installations, fault finding, VSD commissioning and solar PV for residential, commercial and agricultural properties.

George sits at the centre of our Garden Route service area, and it is where a large share of our day-to-day work happens. As a licensed and COC-certified electrician, Johan and the 4C Electrical team work on homes, farms and businesses across the town — from straightforward fault calls to full electrical installations on new builds.

Every job we complete in George is carried out to SANS 10142, the South African standard for the wiring of premises. That means a Certificate of Compliance (COC) is issued for every qualifying installation, which is exactly what you need when selling a property, connecting a new supply, or signing off after major electrical work.

We offer four core services in George:

  • New electrical installations for new builds, renovations and commercial fit-outs.
  • Electrical fault finding — tripping breakers, dead circuits, earth leakage and wiring faults.
  • VSD (Variable Speed Drive) installations for pumps, fans, compressors and conveyor systems.
  • Solar installations — grid-tied and off-grid PV systems for homes and businesses.

We also cover Mossel Bay, Knysna, Wilderness, Sedgefield, Hartenbos, Great Brak River and Albertinia from our George base. Both English and Afrikaans are spoken, payment is by cash or EFT in ZAR, and our office hours are Monday to Friday, 07:00–17:00.
