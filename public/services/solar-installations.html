Solar Installations

Grid-tied and off-grid solar PV systems for homes and businesses across the Garden Route. Every installation is completed by a registered electrician and includes a Certificate of Compliance.

Call 065 865 6575 Request a Quote

4C Electrical designs and installs grid-tied and off-grid solar PV systems for residential and commercial properties across the Garden Route. Reduce your electricity bill, lower your dependence on the grid, and gain back-up through loadshedding — with a compliant, professionally installed system signed off by a registered electrician.

Grid-tied systems work alongside your existing municipal supply and can feed excess power back to the grid where municipal approval allows. They're typically the lower upfront-cost option and suit homes and businesses with reliable grid access. Off-grid systems use batteries and a larger array to provide full energy independence — the right fit for remote farms and properties without practical grid access, and the option that stays on through loadshedding.

Every solar installation includes a Certificate of Compliance (COC) and complies with SANS 10142, Eskom and local municipal requirements. We handle the full job: site assessment, system sizing, mounting, DC and AC cabling, inverter and battery installation where applicable, distribution board integration, and commissioning. Where a solar system is being added to a wider project — a new build or a board upgrade — we co-ordinate the work so it's right from day one.

A recent client in Knysna had us install a grid-tied solar system for their guesthouse — clean installation, on time, all paperwork including the COC handled. We deliver that same standard across George, Mossel Bay, Knysna, Wilderness, Sedgefield, Hartenbos, Great Brak River and Albertinia. Travel charges may apply outside the main service towns.
