New Electrical Installations

COC-certified electrical installations for new builds and renovations — residential, commercial and agricultural projects across George, Mossel Bay, Knysna and the wider Garden Route.

Call 065 865 6575 Request a Quote

4C Electrical provides complete electrical installations for new builds and renovations across the Garden Route. We work on residential homes, commercial properties, and agricultural buildings — and every qualifying installation is signed off with a Certificate of Compliance (COC) under SANS 10142, the South African standard for the wiring of premises.

Whether you're building a new home in George, fitting out a commercial property in Mossel Bay, or wiring an agricultural building outside Knysna, we deliver end-to-end electrical work with attention to safety, compliance and craftsmanship. Skilled electricians using quality, approved materials means the installation works properly the first time — and continues to work reliably afterwards.

A new installation typically covers the full scope: distribution boards, sub-mains, lighting and small-power circuits, earthing and bonding, geyser and appliance connections, and outbuildings. For commercial and agricultural clients we also handle motor circuits and three-phase work. Where a Variable Speed Drive or solar system is part of the build, we integrate it into the design from the start.

Every job is inspected and tested before the COC is signed. A Certificate of Compliance is a legal document required by law when a property is sold, when a new electricity supply is connected, or after major electrical work — so getting it right matters. Johan is a registered electrician qualified to issue COCs across the Garden Route, including George, Mossel Bay, Knysna, Wilderness, Sedgefield, Hartenbos, Great Brak River and Albertinia.
