VSD Installations

Installation and commissioning of Variable Speed Drives for industrial and commercial motor control — pumps, fans, compressors and conveyor systems — across the Garden Route.

Call 065 865 6575 Request a Quote

A Variable Speed Drive (VSD) — also called a variable frequency drive or inverter — is an electronic device that controls the speed of an electric motor by varying the voltage and frequency of the power supply. In industrial and commercial settings VSDs reduce energy consumption, protect motors from mechanical stress, and enable precise process control. 4C Electrical installs and commissions VSDs across the Garden Route.

Common applications include pumps, fans, compressors and conveyor systems. On a Garden Route farm, that often means an irrigation pump that needs to ramp up and down smoothly instead of slamming on and off; on a commercial site it might be HVAC fans or a compressor that's burning more power than it should at full speed. The right VSD, sized and programmed for the load, lowers running cost and extends motor life.

We handle the work end to end: load analysis, drive selection, panel and cabling work, motor protection, commissioning and parameter setup, and the final hand-over showing you how the system behaves under real conditions. Where the installation is part of a wider electrical scope — a new build, a board upgrade, or integration with solar — we co-ordinate it so everything works together. All work meets SANS 10142 and qualifying installations are issued with a Certificate of Compliance.
