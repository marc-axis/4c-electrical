1. Who We Are

4C Electrical ("we", "us", "our") is a licensed electrical contractor operating in the Garden Route, Western Cape, South Africa. Our website is https://4ce.co.za. For any privacy-related queries, contact us at johan@4ce.co.za or call 065 865 6575.

2. What Information We Collect

This website does not use contact forms, user accounts, or cookies for tracking. The only personal information we receive is what you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Call us directly via the telephone link on this site

In those cases, we may receive your name, phone number, email address, and the details of your enquiry.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information solely to:

We do not use your information for marketing without your explicit consent, and we do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

4. POPIA Compliance

We process personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of South Africa. You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at johan@4ce.co.za.
